About me

Although born and raised in Silicon Valley, my coding journey took flight in a vibrant co-work space in Sofia, Bulgaria. Mesmerized by the dance of my friend's espresso-fueled fingers on the keyboard, accompanied by the tunes of Pink Floyd, I sensed a harmonious fusion of artistry and logic that left me captivated. It was in that revelatory moment that I yearned to expand my creative horizons and embarked on a new chapter, influenced by the innovative spirit that shaped my upbringing.

In 2022, fueled by eagerness to broaden my knowledge, I dove into online courses and sought guidance from mentors who helped me carve a path toward becoming a front-end web developer. My ultimate goal is to contribute my skills to a dynamic team, crafting modern web applications that leave a positive impact on lives, no matter how small.

When not immersed in code, you'll find me strumming my guitar, playing classic rock, funk, and R&B. Nature beckons, and I prioritize regular hikes through the beautiful hills of the Bay Area. My spirit finds fulfillment in travel as well—I recently embraced new experiences during a six-month adventure living in Istanbul, Turkey.

This journey weaves together coding, music, nature, and travel, as I explore self-discovery, creativity, and making a difference. The innovative energy of my Silicon Valley upbringing continues to shape my interests and fuel my drive. Join me as I continue to grow, learn, and leave my mark in this ever-changing world.
