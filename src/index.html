About me

My coding journey started at a co-work space in Sofia, Bulgaria, seeing my friend's espresso fueled fingers fly across the keyboard in a flow state, I realized that coding is creative, and I love to create things.

I started digging into online courses in 2022 and picked up a mentor to help me navigate a path to becoming a front end web developer. My intention is to be a part of a team building modern web apps that will help people in some way big or small to to help improve some aspect of their life.

My other interests are playing classic rock, funk and R&B on my guitar. I try get out in nature a few days a week with hikes in the Bay Area hills. Travel always awakens my spirit, my recent adventures led to living in Istanbul, Turkey for 6 months.
